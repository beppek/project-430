About SlideZapp

This app was created as a project by beppek at Linnaeus University. The idea behind the app is to provide an outlet for photographers to grow their skill in a social setting with a little bit of friendly competition.

Challenges are added on a regular basis and they are always open for anyone to join. Members and visitors can then vote for their favorites and watch them climb to the top of the leaderboards.

Members are also able to leave a comment on photos, maybe with a few pointers or simply to give a virtual high five for a job well done. Remember: the purpose is learning.

Membership is of course free and always will be. So don't be shy, join us today and benefit from challenging your photography alongside others.
